Hands-On Chiropractic Care

Precision, delivered by hand.

When a segmental vertebral restriction locks a joint in place, we free it — by hand, with judgment built over years of practice. Every visit begins with an assessment, not a routine.

  • Diversified thrust adjustment — a precise, high-velocity low-amplitude correction to restore motion at a fixed segment.
  • Low-force activator work — a gentler, instrument-assisted option for sensitive spines and joints.
  • Cervical & lumbar mobilization — sustained, graded pressure to ease chronic restriction without a thrust.
  • Myofascial release — hands-on work through the surrounding soft tissue so the correction holds.

Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy

The quiet breath of pressurized repair.

Inside our soft-shell monoplace chamber, ambient pressure rises to 1.3–1.5 ATA — enough to drive transdermal oxygen saturation deep into tissue that ordinary breathing can't reach, and to give hard-working cells the raw material to rebuild.

  • 1.3–1.5 ATA sessions — a gentle, closely monitored pressure range suited to soft-shell chambers.
  • Transdermal oxygen saturation — oxygen delivered through the skin and into the bloodstream under pressure.
  • Angiogenesis support — encouraging new capillary growth into slow-healing tissue.
  • Cellular repair — giving oxygen-starved cells what they need to recover their rhythm.
