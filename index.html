Our Philosophy

The body is architecture. We are its restorers.

Every joint, disc, and nerve pathway is load-bearing. When alignment drifts, the whole structure compensates — tension climbs the spine, breath shortens, focus dulls. Hands On Wellness exists to correct that drift at its source, not mask it downstream.

We treat the nervous system as the body's central wiring and the vertebral column as its conduit. Chiropractic adjustment restores mechanical order; hyperbaric oxygen therapy floods the recovering tissue with the raw material it needs to rebuild. Used together, they address structure and cellular repair as one continuous system, not two separate appointments.

No shortcuts. No overselling. Just precise, evidence-informed care delivered with a steady hand.

Modalities

Four disciplines.
One restorative system.

Each modality is delivered as its own precise intervention — sequenced, measured, and adapted to where your body is holding tension or lacking oxygen.

01

Chiropractic Adjustment

Manual and instrument-assisted spinal manipulation to correct vertebral subluxation, restore joint mobility, and relieve nerve interference along the spinal column. Sessions are assessed individually via postural and motion analysis before any adjustment is made.

  • Vertebral alignment & joint mobilization
  • Nervous system regulation
  • Postural correction protocols
02

Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy

Pressurized, oxygen-rich chambers increase the amount of oxygen your blood plasma can carry — accelerating cellular oxygenation, reducing inflammation, and supporting tissue regeneration well beyond what breathing at normal atmospheric pressure can achieve.

  • Elevated cellular oxygenation
  • Inflammation & recovery support
  • Monitored, single-occupancy chambers
03

Spinal Decompression

Gentle, controlled traction creates negative pressure within the disc space — encouraging retraction of herniated or bulging material, improving circulation to the area, and relieving pressure on compressed nerve roots without surgery.

  • Non-surgical disc relief
  • Nerve root decompression
  • Progressive, tracked sessions
04

Wellness Consultation

A full intake — postural assessment, movement screening, and history review — used to build a sequenced care plan that combines adjustment, decompression, and hyperbaric sessions in the order your body actually needs them.

  • Postural & movement screening
  • Individualized care sequencing
  • Ongoing progress review

Benefits & Science

Two disciplines, one physiological outcome.

Chiropractic adjustment removes mechanical interference along the spinal column, allowing the nervous system to communicate with the rest of the body without obstruction. Hyperbaric oxygen therapy raises dissolved oxygen levels in the blood plasma, reaching tissue that reduced circulation would otherwise starve.

Independently, each modality supports recovery. Together, sequenced deliberately, they compound: a structurally aligned body oxygenates more efficiently, and an oxygen-rich tissue bed heals faster from the corrective work of adjustment.
